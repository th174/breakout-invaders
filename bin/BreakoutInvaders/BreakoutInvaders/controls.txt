Mousemove:  move player ship
                                               Mouseclick: release Plasmaball
                                               Spacebar:   pausegame

                                               Debug keys:
                                               B:          spawn new ball
                                               H:          max HP
                                               M:          toggle magnetic paddle (balls move to center of paddle instead of bouncing away, unless released)
                                               N:          instantly kill all enemies in level
